I'm Zaheda & this is my passion.


I care about people, too much infact, I think it's more accurate to say I love people.

My educational background is in psychology, I have 11 years of corporate experience in the Digital sector & am experienced in Life and Holistic counseling.

Holistic healing involves physical, mental, social & spiritual aspects. You're not going to leave my sessions with a pill that will 'fix' your problems or numb you from them.

My aim is to understand and empathize, then give you a set of tools to unleash your limitless potential.


Your life and your circumstances are as vivid and complex as my own, & I'd like to help you understand yourself better.
